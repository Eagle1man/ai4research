# Example Prompts for AI4Research

## 1. Literature Understanding (AI4SC)
"Summarize the following abstract and extract key methods, parameters, and main results."

## 2. Automated Review Generation (AI4AS)
"Based on the retrieved papers, generate a structured literature review with subsections: Introduction, Methods, Results, Discussion."

## 3. Hypothesis Generation (AI4SD)
"Propose 5 testable hypotheses to improve material X based on the described results and historical trends. Include rationale and suggested experiment."

## 4. Writing Assistance (AI4AW)
"Generate a scientific abstract in academic style summarizing the core findings of this study."

## 5. Peer Review Automation (AI4APR)
"Evaluate this paper according to novelty, methodology, reproducibility, and clarity. Output a review with pros and cons."
